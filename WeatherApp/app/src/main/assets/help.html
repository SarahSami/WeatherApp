Weather App Tutorial!

How to use:

 - You can add city by clicking on the rounded button on the first screen and it will redirect you to map screen where you can click on any location to add the city.

- click on any city from the main screen to see weather details and forecast for the next 5 days.

- you can always delete any city by clicking on 'Remove' button from cities list.


Enjoy the weather app. It is all FREE!
